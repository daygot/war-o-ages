The Council banner
Four replacements for the round-table graphic at the head of the War Council. All use your real crests and names — the shields here are stand-ins. Pick one and I'll build it.
A · The Hung Standards five crests hang as pennants from a single lance shaft — the legion at rest before council. dark, ceremonial, very "war tent"
B · The Commander's Arc crests fan in a shallow arc around the commander, who sits center and larger — hierarchy at a glance, lots of presence
C · The Sealed Charter a parchment muster-roll: legion name in lettering, five wax seals pressed along the foot — ties into choosing an ideology (you're signing the charter)
D · The March Column the five standards on the move, left to right on a ground line — echoes the cartouche footer's etched style; quiet and atmospheric
